Michael Jordan

Air Jordan - The greatest basketball player of all time

Michael Jordan is one of the greatest players in the history of basketball. He led the Chicago Bulls to six NBA championships and won the MVP Award five times.
By the late 1980s, the Chicago Bulls were quickly becoming a force to be reckoned with, and Jordan was an instrumental part of the team's success.

Michael Jordan's Career highlights and awards

  • (1991–1993, 1996–1998) - 6× NBA champion
  • (1991–1993, 1996–1998) - 6× NBA Finals MVP
  • (1988, 1991, 1992, 1996, 1998) - 5× NBA Most Valuable Player
  • (1985–1993, 1996–1998, 2002, 2003) - 14× NBA All-Star
  • (1988, 1996, 1998) - 3× NBA All-Star Game MVP
  • (1987–1993, 1996–1998) - 10× All-NBA First Team
  • (1985) - All-NBA Second Team
  • (1988–1993, 1996–1998) - 9× NBA All-Defensive First Team
  • (1985) - NBA Rookie of the Year
  • (1987–1993, 1996–1998) - 10× NBA scoring champion
  • (1987, 1988) - 2× NBA Slam Dunk Contest champion
  • No. 23 - retired by Chicago Bulls & Miami Heat
  • (1991, 1992, 1993) - 3× AP Athlete of the Year
  • (1983, 1984) - 2× USA Basketball Male Athlete of the Year
